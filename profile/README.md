# Neuroframe

Neuroframe helps companies move from personal AI to company AI.

Most businesses already have employees using ChatGPT, Claude, Copilot, and other AI tools. The knowledge, prompts, and workflows they build often remain trapped inside personal accounts.

Neuroframe provides a private AI system that connects company knowledge, workflows, and communication into a secure, company-owned environment.

## Super

Super is AI that remembers how your company works.

- Participates in conversations and projects across your organization
- Builds context around your company's knowledge, tools, and workflows over time
- Runs automated tasks on schedules or in response to organizational events
- Connects to the services your team already uses through secure integrations
- Responds to real-time activity across connected systems
- Enables collaboration between your team and your Super in a shared workspace

## Connections

Super integrates with GitHub, Gmail, Outlook, Google Calendar, Google Drive, Notion, Airtable, Dropbox, Brex, Affinity, Granola, X, and more.
